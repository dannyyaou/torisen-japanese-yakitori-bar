Our Story

The Art of Yakitori

At Torisen Japanese yakitori & bar, we honour the centuries-old tradition of Japanese charcoal grilling. Each skewer is carefully prepared, seasoned with our house tare sauce, and grilled over binchotan charcoal to achieve the perfect smoky char.

From premium chicken thigh to tender tsukune meatballs, our yakitori is crafted with the same dedication found in the backstreet izakayas of Tokyo. Paired with carefully selected sake, Japanese whisky, and craft cocktails, we bring an authentic slice of Japan to Birmingham.
